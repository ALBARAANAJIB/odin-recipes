Shawarma!

Description

Shawarma, a beloved Middle Eastern delight, features thinly sliced marinated meat - chicken, beef, or lamb - cooked on a vertical rotisserie. Bursting with flavors from spices like cumin, coriander, and garlic

Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced chicken, beef, or lamb
  • Pita or flatbread wraps
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Paprika
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Tahini sauce or garlic sauce

Steps

  1. Marinate the thinly sliced meat with cumin, coriander, paprika, garlic, and lemon juice.
  2. Stack the marinated meat onto a vertical rotisserie and cook until crispy on the outside and tender on the inside.
  3. Warm the pita or flatbread wraps.
  4. Assemble the shawarma wraps by placing the cooked meat along with lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and your choice of tahini sauce or garlic sauce onto the warm wraps.
  5. Roll up the wraps and enjoy!
