Chicken Fritta

Description

Picture this: tender chicken pieces, coated in a crispy golden crust, perfectly seasoned and fried to delicious perfection. Chicken fritta is a delightful dish that combines the savory flavors of juicy chicken with the satisfying crunch of a well-seasoned coating.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast (cut into bite-sized pieces)
  • All-purpose flour
  • Eggs (beaten)
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

Steps

  1. Preheat vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Cut chicken breasts into strips or bite-sized pieces.
  3. In three separate bowls, place flour, beaten eggs, and breadcrumbs mixed with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
  4. Dredge chicken pieces in flour, then dip in beaten eggs, and finally coat with breadcrumb mixture.
  5. Place coated chicken pieces in the hot oil and fry until golden brown and cooked through, about 4-5 minutes per side.
  6. Remove cooked chicken fritta from oil and drain on paper towels.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
