Easy Baked Beef Brisket

Description

Indulge in the savory symphony of flavors with our baked beef brisket. Slow-cooked to perfection, each tender slice of beef boasts a tantalizing blend of aromatic spices and rich juices, creating a culinary masterpiece that melts in your mouth. Our signature baking process locks in the succulence, ensuring each bite is an explosion of hearty goodness. Whether it's the centerpiece of a family feast or the star of a casual dinner, our baked beef brisket promises a delectable experience that satisfies every craving.

Ingredients needed!

  • Beef brisket
  • Aromatic spices (such as garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, cumin, and black pepper)
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Beef broth or stock
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Dijon mustard
  • Apple cider vinegar

Steps(make sure to follow carefully)

  1. Preheat your oven to the desired temperature, typically around 300°F (150°C).
  2. Prepare the brisket by trimming excess fat, then pat it dry with paper towels.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together your aromatic spices, salt, and any additional seasonings you desire.
  4. Rub the spice mixture generously all over the brisket, ensuring even coverage.
  5. Heat olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet or roasting pan over medium-high heat.
  6. Sear the brisket on all sides until nicely browned, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  7. Remove the brisket from the skillet and set it aside.
  8. In the same skillet, deglaze with beef broth or stock, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom.
  9. Stir in Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, Dijon mustard, and apple cider vinegar to create a flavorful sauce.
  10. Return the brisket to the skillet, nestling it into the sauce.
  11. Cover the skillet or roasting pan with foil or a lid.
  12. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for several hours, or until the brisket is fork-tender.
  13. Once done, remove the brisket from the oven and let it rest for about 15-20 minutes before slicing.
  14. Serve the sliced brisket with the delicious pan sauce drizzled over the top.
